## 5. App-critique interview framework prompts (typical at Meta, Reddit, etc.)

1. Tell me about your favorite app — what works and what doesn't?
2. Pick an app you use daily — how would you customize it without breaking it?
3. Compare two competing apps in the same category — which wins on UX and why?
4. Identify three dark patterns in a popular app — propose ethical alternatives.
5. What's one feature that should never have shipped, and why?

## 6. Behavioral / portfolio questions (recurring across companies)

- Tell me about your background in design and recent projects
- What made you get into design?
- What problem did you solve with this project?
- What metrics signaled you to work on this problem?
- If you could do this project again, what would you do differently?
- What guided your assumptions and hypotheses?
- What was the biggest failure of this project?
- Why this company?
- Why product designer rather than UX researcher?
- Walk me through your workflow
- What research methods do you use?
- How do you respond to negative feedback?
- Tell me about your most/least successful project
- Tell me about a time you disagreed with a product decision — what happened?
- Describe a stressful situation at work and how you handled it
- Tell me about a time when a project didn't go as planned — how did you fix it?
- What would you do if asked to hand the project over to a developer?
- How would you describe your design thinking?
- Describe a time you resolved a conflict among team members on a design project
- Share an experience where your leadership improved cross-team collaboration

## 8. Take-home challenge prompts (typically 4–8h, 1 week to deliver)

- **ProPhoto** — Design a photo platform distinct from Instagram, VSCO, Snapchat
- **Roomies** — Secure roommate-matching app for US millennials
- **Educator Price-Comparison** — Sortable list view for teacher supplies
- **Google Sales Funnel** — Funnel management inside Enterprise Gmail
- **Podcast Merch Landing** — Page for an AI shipping tool for podcasters
- **Restaurant Recs Settings** — Cuisine + dietary preference customization
- **E-commerce Upselling** — Strategy that doesn't disrupt checkout
- **Gym Class Checkout** — Purchasing for individual fitness classes
- **Cleaning-Staff App** — Detailed map component for janitorial tracking
- **SmartHome Voice Assistant** — Caregiver interface for remote patient monitoring
- **GoPro for Gen-Z** — Reimagine the mobile app beyond photo sharing
- **Green Bike Challenge** — Timed exercise (~5h)
- **Rock Climbing Instruction Manual** — Off-domain design challenge

## 9. Practical micro-exercises (for warm-up / portfolio)

- Design a blog post for a fashion website
- Design a pop-up for a special offer
- Redesign Craigslist
- Microinteraction: deleting an item / downloading a video
- Zero-UI pizza ordering (voice + gesture only)
- AI-only design — use only prompts, no manual design
- Dark-pattern reversal — design a manipulative flow, then redesign ethically
- 50+ category card sort for a content-heavy product
